# VoiScribe
Offline **Voi**ce Tran**scribe** iOS Application

Introducing VoiScribe, a voice recognition app run on your smartphone.

With VoiScribe, there's no need to worry about your information being compromised by other voice recognition apps.

This app ensures your important meetings and confidential conversations remain just that - confidential.

## App Store
- https://apps.apple.com/us/app/voiscribe/id1668093608

## Prerequisite
- [whisper.cpp](https://github.com/ggerganov/whisper.cpp) model is required.
- Download whisper.cpp model below and rename each model.
  - [small-en-8bit-quantized](models.openly.jp/ggml-small.en.q8_0.bin) -> Rename to "ggml-small.en.bin"
  - [small-multi-8bit-quantized](models.openly.jp/ggml-small.q8_0.bin) -> Rename to "ggml-small.multi.bin"

## Key Features
- Voice Recording
- Offline Automatic Transcription in English and Japanese
- Ability to Edit Transcription Results

## Recommended Devices
- iPhone 11 and newer models (with at least 4GB of RAM)
- iPad Air (2020), iPad Pro 11 inch (2018), iPad Pro 12.9 inch (2018), iPad mini (2021), and newer models.
